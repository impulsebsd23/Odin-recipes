Delicious Greek Honey Cake

This Greek Honey Cake is pure comfort in every bite. Soft, tender, and delicately spiced, this golden cake is soaked in a warm honey syrup that seeps into every crumb, creating an irresistibly moist texture. Notes of cinnamon and citrus brighten the richness, while a glossy honey glaze on top adds the perfect touch of sweetness. It’s simple enough for a cozy weeknight treat, yet elegant enough to serve at gatherings. Each slice is fragrant, lightly sweet, and beautifully balanced — the kind of timeless dessert that feels like it’s been passed down for generations.
