Savory Chicken Roast

This spicy rapid chicken roast will knock your socks off. All you will need is a hot oven and a empty belly

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
